Why George Brown College?

1 - Study downtown: Our locations are strategically placed to give you access to top employers for field education purposes.
2 - Hands-on, real-world experience: Facilities include the Chef's House and WAVE Clinics, simulating real-world employment settings. 
3 - Informed by Industry: We work with industry leaders to ensure the skills you learn in class are the ones you need for the job.
4 - Expert Instructors: Most faculty members have industry experience and will share real-world knowledge with you.
5 - Applied Research: Gain hands-on experience working alongside industry professionals to help develop and test new products.
6 - Promote Entrepreneurship - Want to start your own business? GBC's entrepreneurship hub, starGBC, can help you get your idea off the ground.
